# Unit Economics: Credex AI Audit

## 1. Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
* **Monetization Model**: 10% commission on the "identified annual savings" if the user switches plans via Credex.
* **Average Identified Savings**: $2,400/year per startup.
* **Revenue per Conversion**: $240 (one-time referral/commission).
* **Retention/Upsell**: 20% of users move to the full Credex SaaS platform ($100/mo subscription).
* **Total Estimated LTV**: ~$480 (weighted average).

## 2. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
* **Channel**: LinkedIn Ads / Cold Outreach Automation.
* **Cost per Click (CPC)**: $4.00.
* **Landing Page Conversion**: 15%.
* **Lead to Customer Conversion**: 10%.
* **Total Estimated CAC**: $26.60.

## 3. LTV : CAC Ratio
* **Ratio**: ~18:1.
* **Verdict**: Extremely healthy. Anything above 3:1 is considered a strong venture-scale business.

## 4. Operational Costs (COGS)
* **Hosting**: Vercel (Free/Pro Tier) - $20/mo.
* **Database**: Supabase (Free Tier) - $0.
* **AI API (OpenAI/Gemini)**: ~$0.05 per audit.
* **Gross Margin**: >98%.

## 5. Scalability
The "Audit Engine" is fully automated. Whether we audit 10 startups or 10,000, the marginal cost of the next audit is near zero. This provides a high-leverage entry point for Credex to capture the entire startup spend market.
