# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy: Credex AI Audit

## 1. Target Audience
* **Primary**: Seed to Series A tech startups (Team size: 10-50).
* **Persona**: CTOs and Ops Managers who are seeing "AI tool sprawl" (multiple subscriptions to ChatGPT, Claude, and Cursor across the team).
* **Pain Point**: Spending $1,000+/mo on redundant AI seats without a clear ROI.

## 2. Acquisition Channels
### A. LinkedIn "Spend Shame" Campaign (Organic)
* **Strategy**: Post screenshots of the "Identified Leaks" dashboard from our tool.
* **Hook**: "Is your team paying for both GitHub Copilot and Cursor? You're leaking $20/seat. Our free audit found $400/mo in waste for a 10-person team."

### B. The "Savings Roadmap" Lead Magnet (Paid)
* **Strategy**: Run targeted LinkedIn ads at CTOs. 
* **Call to Action**: "Get your 30-day AI Savings Roadmap in 2 minutes."
* **Conversion**: Capture email + company size to unlock the PDF report.

### C. Founder Communities (Partnerships)
* **Strategy**: Partner with YC/Techstars alumni groups.
* **Value Prop**: Offer a "Batch Audit" for all portfolio companies to reduce their collective burn rate.

## 3. Viral Loop: The "Optimized" Badge
* **Mechanism**: Upon reaching a "90+ Audit Score" (low waste), users are prompted to share a "Lean Startup" badge on Twitter/LinkedIn.
* **Impact**: Each share includes the unique audit URL, driving peer CTOs back to the platform to check their own score.

## 4. Sales Motion
* **Low Savings (<$100)**: Self-service. User follows the automated checklist.
* **High Savings (>$500)**: Prompt for a 15-min "Credex Strategy Call." This funnels high-value leads into Credex's core marketplace negotiation service.
